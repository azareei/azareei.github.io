I am currently postdoctoral fellow in Applied Mathematics in School of Engineering and Applied Sciences at Harvard University working jointly with Professors Katia Bertoldi and Ariel Amir

I got my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering at UC Berkeley. I was advised by Professor M. Reza Alam in Theoretical and Applied Fluids group at UC Berkeley where my work was mainly focused on nonlinear wave dynamics. My research was funded in part by National Science Foundation and I was PI for Berkeley Fluids Seminar from 2015 - 2018.
